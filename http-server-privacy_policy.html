Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the "http server" app ("we", "our", "app") handles information.

Summary

  • • We do not collect, sell, or share personal information.
  • • The app serves files from your device over your local network only.

Information We Collect

We do not collect or send any personal data to our servers. The app does not use analytics or advertising SDKs.

Permissions and Local Data

To function correctly, the app requires specific permissions used strictly for technical purposes:

  • INTERNET Used to host a local HTTP server on your device.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE Used to keep the server running in the background.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS Used to show a notification while the server is active.

Files You Choose

If you use "Import Website", the app reads files from the folder you select and copies them into the app's private storage on your device. This data stays 100% on your device and is never uploaded.

Local Network Access

When the server is running, devices on the same local network can access the files you choose to host. This traffic stays on your local network and is not sent to us.

Data Retention

All app data is stored locally and can be removed by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app from your device.

Changes

We may update this policy. We will update the effective date at the top of this page when changes are made.
